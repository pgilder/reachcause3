Nutritious Food Changes Everything

Whether it is distributing Emergency Food Boxes during the holidays, providing nutritious, vitamin-rich, and vegetables, or helping underfed wildlife through member agency programs, Wild Animal Sanctuary Fund is dedicated to relieving hunger to as many of our nation’s neglected wildlife as we can. Last year alone, Wild Animal Sanctuary Fund provided 13 regional food rescue organizations in 9 states with over 1.1 million pounds of food.
